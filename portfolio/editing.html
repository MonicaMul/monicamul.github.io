Editing Samples

Below you will find screenshots as well as links to the final versions of student profiles that I helped edit. When a Student Profile is written, it goes through three different editors: myself, my supervisor, and our journalism intern. Each week an email is sent out with the drafts attached. The editors then read for grammar and content. When there is a grammar mistake that needs to be corrected, the correction is added and then highlighted. Content edits are highlighted as well with the editor's comments being included in parenthesis. I unfortunately do not have copies of the unedited versions.
